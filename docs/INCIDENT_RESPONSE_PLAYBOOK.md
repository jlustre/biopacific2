# Incident Response & Breach Notification Plan

## 1. Purpose
This document outlines the steps to take in the event of a security incident or data breach, including notification procedures and key contacts.

## 2. Incident Response Playbook

### Step 1: Identification
- Detect and confirm the incident (monitoring, alerts, user reports).
- Log the date, time, and nature of the incident.

### Step 2: Containment
- Isolate affected systems to prevent further damage.
- Change passwords and revoke access if necessary.

### Step 3: Assessment
- Determine the scope and impact (data affected, systems, users).
- Collect evidence and preserve logs.

### Step 4: Eradication & Recovery
- Remove the cause of the incident (malware, unauthorized access).
- Restore systems from clean backups.
- Monitor for signs of recurrence.

### Step 5: Notification
- Notify affected parties as required by law (see below).
- Notify internal stakeholders and legal/compliance teams.

### Step 6: Post-Incident Review
- Document the incident, response, and lessons learned.
- Update policies and controls as needed.

## 3. Breach Notification Plan
- Notify affected individuals without unreasonable delay.
- Notify regulatory authorities (e.g., HHS for HIPAA) within required timeframes.
- Provide details: nature of breach, data involved, steps taken, contact info.
- Use email, phone, and/or public notice as appropriate.
